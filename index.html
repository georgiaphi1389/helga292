Are you a hero, villain, or something in between?
I think that Helga is cemented pretty solidly as a hero... two OTTP elimination episodes anyone?? Between my natural approach to the game and being forced into an underdog position in Alsius, being seen as a hero was inevitable. In Atlantis, I definitely started the game with some mixed tone as I tried to play from a power position. Ultimately, however, I still received a lot of PSPV and ended up playing from the minority at the end... so hero still yup.
What was your shining moment in Good and Evil?
Honestly wow, there were just so many of them tbh. I have to say that going deep alias in my first season and just having those absolutely ridiculous conversations with people was very fun and different for me. It was really exciting to not be myself?? So getting the chance to build relationships with everyone and create this over-the-top, enigmatic persona sticks out to me. If I had to pick a more specific moment, I feel like rallying the troops against Atohi to start G&E12 was probably that moment where everything changed. Helga went from a cute underdog to a much stronger strategic force, and the second the switch was flipped, there was no going back.

How will you approach your Legacy in the game?

I want to defend my Legacy. My usual mindset going into an All Stars game is that I'd really love a blank slate with everyone... the past should remain the past in a perfect world. Of course, I think humans naturally fall back into learned behavior, and they won't forget the legacy that a certain player holds. Quite honestly, I'm a bit concerned about my chances in this season given the legacy that Helga has in this series. Even though I never made a final tribal council, I was a pretty central, popular presence in both of my seasons. Everyone was like omg Helga is such a threat, we just can't take her to the end!! And then of course, I've been ranked #1 in the character rankings, which has always been an incredible honor. My concern here is that people would look at me and be like... this Russian bitch has to GO. I wouldn't put it past some of these people to try and tear others down just so they can improve their legacies lmao, so as much as I don't want to, I anticipate that I will have to play from a defensive position pretty early on.

What Survivor player would you compare Helga to?

That's an INTERESTING question. From a storyline perspective, I'm trying to think of a player who was a big underdog character in their first season, and then became much more of a power player in their second go-around. I guess Tasha/Spencer from Cagayan/Cambodia kind of fit that mold, but I think I absolutely had the jury on my side in Atlantis... whereas Tasha/Spencer did! not! Honestly, Cirie might be another good comparison given her ability to flame out right at the end of the game multiple times.
